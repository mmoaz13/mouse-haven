Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our gaming mice.

What is the best gaming mouse?

It depends on your preference, but some top choices are Razer DeathAdder, Logitech G Pro, and SteelSeries Rival. The best mouse for you depends on your hand size, grip style, and gaming preferences.

Shop Gaming Mice
Do wireless mice have lag?

Most modern wireless mice use advanced technology like 2.4GHz and Bluetooth, reducing lag to an almost unnoticeable level. Premium gaming wireless mice often have response times comparable to or better than wired alternatives.

Shop Wireless Mice
What is DPI and why is it important?

DPI (dots per inch) determines the sensitivity of your mouse. Higher DPI means faster cursor movement with less physical movement, while lower DPI provides more precision for tasks requiring fine control. Most gaming mice allow you to adjust DPI settings to match different games and scenarios.

Shop High-DPI Mice
How long do gaming mice typically last?

Quality gaming mice can last 3-5 years with regular use. Factors affecting lifespan include build quality, switch durability, and how intensively you use the mouse. Premium mice often feature optical switches rated for 70+ million clicks.
